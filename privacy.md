## 2. Personal information we collect
We collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to operate our services, including:

### 2.1 Contact data
- Name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Business name
- Job title

### 2.2 Service usage data
- Call details (caller name, number, time, call outcome)
- Messages taken during calls
- Appointment or booking information
- Communication preferences

### 2.3 Website data
- IP address
- Browser information
- Pages visited
- Device information
- Referral information

### 2.4 Payment data
If you purchase services, we may collect:
- Billing contact details
- Business address
- *Note: Payments may be processed by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) and we do not store full card details.*

## 3. How we collect personal information
We may collect information:
- Directly from customers during onboarding
- During inbound and outbound calls
- Via our website forms
- Via emails and messages
- From third-party booking or payment platforms integrated by customers
- From analytics platforms when browsing our website

We may also receive information when customers provide their client lists, calendars, or CRM access for receptionist services.

## 4. Why we collect personal information
We collect personal information to:
- Deliver virtual receptionist and call-handling services
- Manage bookings, message forwarding, and call routing
- Provide customer support and onboarding
- Process payments and manage accounts
- Improve our website, products, and services
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- Communicate updates, service changes, and marketing (opt-out available)

## 5. How we use and disclose personal information
We may disclose personal information to:
- Receptionists and service agents
- Cloud hosting, telephony, and communication providers
- Payment processors
- CRM, scheduling, or ticketing platforms used by customers
- IT, cybersecurity, or data backup providers
- Legal or regulatory authorities if required

**We do not sell personal information to third parties.**

## 6. Overseas disclosure
Some service providers we use may store data overseas. Common regions include:
- United States
- European Union
- Singapore

Where data is stored overseas, we take reasonable steps to ensure privacy protections are applied.

## 7. Direct marketing
We may send marketing communications about our services. You may opt out at any time by:
- Using unsubscribe links in email communication, or
- Contacting us directly at: info@receptionists.net.au

## 8. Cookies and analytics
We may use cookies and analytics tools to:
- Improve website performance
- Analyse traffic and usage patterns
- Personalise content

These tools may collect pseudonymised data such as IP addresses, device details, and browsing behavior.

## 9. Data security
We take reasonable steps to safeguard personal information from:
- Unauthorised access
- Loss or theft
- Misuse or interference
- Modification or disclosure

Security measures may include:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Access controls and authentication
- Staff confidentiality agreements
- Data backups and monitoring

*However, no online system is entirely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.*

## 10. Data retention
We retain personal information for as long as necessary to:
- Deliver services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce agreements

When no longer required, information may be securely destroyed or de-identified.

## 11. Access and correction
